Slice of Life Shows

Twenty Five Twenty One

Release:Feb 12, 2022
Episodes: 16
Cast:Kim Tae-ri, Nam Joo-hyuk, Bona (Kim Ji-yeon), Choi Hyun-wook, Lee Joo-myung

Set during the 1997 IMF crisis, the drama follows a passionate fencer, Na Hee-do, and a young man rebuilding his life, Baek Yi-jin. Through friendship, love, and dreams, the two grow together as they navigate hardships, meeting at ages 22 and 18, and again at 25 and 21.

Reply 1988

Release: Nov 6, 2015
Episodes: 20
Cast: Hyeri, Park Bo-gum, Ryu Jun-yeol Go Kyung-pyo, Lee Dong-hwi

A nostalgic slice-of-life drama set in 1988, centered on five childhood friends and their families living in the same neighborhood in Seoul. It explores friendship, first love, family bonds, and the warmth of community life during simpler times.

When life gives you Tangerines

Release: Jan 01, 2025
Episodes: 12
Cast: IU (Lee Ji-eun), Park Bo-gum, Moon So-ri, Park Hae-joon

A healing melodrama set on Jeju Island during the 1950s–2000s, following the life and love story of Ae-sun, a woman with big dreams, and Gwan-shik, a man of few words but steadfast devotion. Spanning decades, the drama explores resilience, gratitude, and the bittersweet passage of time—symbolized by Jeju’s iconic tangerines.

My Mister

Release: Mar 21, 2018
Episodes: 16
Cast: Lee Sun-kyun, IU (Lee Ji-eun), Park Ho-san, Song Sae-byeok

A deeply moving drama about three middle-aged brothers facing struggles in life and a young woman burdened with hardships. Through an unexpected connection between Dong-hoon and Ji-an, both find healing, compassion, and a chance to rediscover hope.
