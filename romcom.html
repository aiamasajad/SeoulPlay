Romance Shows

Hometown Cha-Cha-Cha

Release: Aug 28, 2021
Episodes: 16
Cast: Shin Min-a, Kim Seon-ho

A heartwarming romantic comedy set in the picturesque seaside village of Gongjin. Yoon Hye-jin, a perfectionist city dentist, moves to the village and meets Hong Du-sik, a jack-of-all-trades beloved by the locals. Despite their differences, sparks fly, and their relationship slowly blossoms.

Business Proposal

Release: Feb 28, 2022
Episodes: 12
Cast: Ahn Hyo-seop, Kim Se-jeong, Kim Min-kyu, Seol In-ah

A romantic comedy about Shin Ha-ri, an office worker who goes on a blind date in place of her friend — only to find that her date is her company’s CEO, Kang Tae-moo. Chaos and comedy ensue as they fake a relationship for business reasons but start catching real feelings.

True Beauty

Release: Dec 09, 2020
Episodes: 16
Cast: Moon Ga-young, Cha Eun-woo, Hwang In-yeop, Park Yoo-na

Lim Ju-kyung is a high school student who masters makeup to hide her bare face and gain confidence. Torn between two boys — Su-ho, the cold perfectionist, and Seo-jun, the rebellious sweetheart — she navigates love, friendship, and self-acceptance.

King the Land

Release: Jun 17, 2023
Episodes: 16
Cast: Lee Jun-ho, Im Yoon-ah

Gu Won is the heir to a luxury hotel conglomerate who despises fake smiles. Sa-rang is a bright and hardworking hotel employee who always wears one. When their paths cross, a romance begins to bloom in the glamorous yet competitive hotel.
