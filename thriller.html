Thriller Shows

Flower of Evil

Release: Jul 29, 2022
Episodes: 16
Cast: Lee Joon-gi, Moon Chae-won, Seo Hyun-won, Jang Hee-jin,

A gripping thriller and melodrama about Baek Hee-sung, a seemingly perfect husband and father who is actually hiding a dark past and a new identity. His wife, a homicide detective, begins to suspect him as she investigates a series of murders connected to his history.

Vigilante

Release: Nov 8, 2023
Episodes: 8
Cast: Nam Joo-hyuk, Yoo Ji-tae, Lee Joon-hyuk, Kim So-jin,

Based on the webtoon, the story follows Kim Ji-yong, a police academy student by day and a vigilante by night. He punishes criminals who escape the law, gaining media attention and forcing society to question justice, morality, and power.

Weak Hero Class 1

Release: Nov 18, 2022
Episodes: 8
Cast: Park Ji-hoon, Choi Hyun-wook, Hong Kyung,

Adapted from the hit webtoon, this action youth drama tells the story of Yeon Si-eun, a top student who appears physically weak but uses intelligence, strategy, and psychology to fight back against school bullies. With new friends, he learns about loyalty, pain, and survival.

Save Me

Release: Aug 5, 2017
Episodes: 16
Cast: Seo Yea-ji, Ok Taec-yeon, Woo Do-hwan, Jo Sung-ha

A psychological thriller about a young woman trapped in a pseudo-religious cult after her family’s misfortune. She desperately seeks help, and four young men from her town try to save her while uncovering the cult’s sinister secrets.
